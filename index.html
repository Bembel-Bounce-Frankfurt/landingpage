Covid-19 Update

Due to the current situation, we want to give you an update on the status of Bembel Bounce.

At this moment we’re still intending for Bembel Bounce to happen as planned from September 4th to September 6th 2020.

But we’re going to put things on hold for a while and keep an eye on the Covid-19/Coronavirus situation. We consider the health of our attendees our top priority and will base our decision primarily on this factor.

However, nothing is decided yet. Stay calm, don’t share your Bembel! We’ll keep you posted!
